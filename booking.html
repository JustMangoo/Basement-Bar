What The Basement has to offer:

Basement is the place to go to enjoy a coffee, drink a beer or enjoy a non-alcoholic beverage with your fellow students, or just use the facilities as a quiet study area during the week.

If you want to host an event or have a party for your fellow students, you can use Basement as your location.

Basement can be adjusted to meet the needs of different types of events, with a flexible set-up for lighting, speakers/music and interior. You can also play various board games, PlayStation, table football, beer pong or shuffleboard.

We look forward to seeing you!
